OUR PROJECTS

ZAMBIAN PROJECTS


KASUMBALESA PROJECT:

We were the main contractors on this project it involved 130,000m3 of bush clearing, leveling and preparation for the construction of an industrial area. With our many efficient machineries which included: A 36 tons Excavator, A Roller compactor, 10 Tipper Trucks, Two ADT 30 Tons Dump Trucks, Two Front End Loaders, A TLB, A Dozer, Two Graders and Two water bowsers, we were able to execute and complete this project in 6 months.


MARLEX MINING PROJECT:

This was a 1 and half year project which involved Earth works in an open pit mine. A 36 Tons Excavator, Dozer D8, Chain Loader, Two Front End Loaders, TLB, Rock Break Hammers and 2 ATD Dump Trucks were used to execute works during the duration of the project.


KALA PROJECT:

We have been sub-contractors on this project since 2018. This project involves: bush clearing, earth works in preparation for the construction of a residential area and all the facilities.


LUFWANYAMA PROJECT:

This project involves bush clearing and the construction of a 5,000,000m3 water reservation and a Dam for agriculture purposes.


CHISANGA FARM PROJECT:

We are main contractors on this project that involved the bush clearing and development of a 2000 hectares' farm includes: internal roads, bush clearing, preparation for agriculture and more.
