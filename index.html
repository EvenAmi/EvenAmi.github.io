ABOUT US

Even Ami Civil Engineering Ltd was established in Zambia in 2017 as a subsidiary of Even Ami Civil Engineering Ltd from Israel. Even Ami is in the infrastructure and heavy construction hiring business with hedquarter in Jerusalem and a branch in the heart of the copprbelt in the city of Kitwe.

Even Ami has everything necessary to carry out and lead successfully any project, of any type and of any scope. The company's vast expertise and her long-standing relationships constitute the solid foundation that enables the execution of work at the highest level.

Since the company established it has carried out numerous projects within the infrastructure, earthmoving, agriculture and the mining fields.
